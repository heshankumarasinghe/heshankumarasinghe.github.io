School of Foundation Studies

The School of Foundation Studies offers programmes that are designed to get young students started in their education journey in PSB Academy. These programmes will provide students with the knowledge as well as interpersonal skills, linguistic skills and general competence upon which to prepare the students for future education.

Lessons within each course are designed with experiential learning journeys and interactive activities that focus on different subjects and skills. The School also runs study tours and short-term language skill workshops.

School of Engineering and Technology

The School of Engineering and Technology (SET) drives the future by shaping elite generations of global nation-builders and digital think tanks. In line with the rapid worldwide growth of the STEM sector, our full-time and part-time courses are designed to ensure that students are equipped with relevant skills and are industry-ready once they graduate from their respective specialised courses.

Specialisations offered range from mechanical and electrical engineering, to information technology and cybersecurity, to environmental and occupational health and safety, catering to various key areas of interest within the engineering and technology industry. SET courses provide an opportunity for our students to apply their theoretical knowledge in a practical hands-on setting with well-equipped engineering and computer labs and facilities, preparing them for real-world challenges and scenarios.

If you are an individual aspiring to take the lead in pushing the boundaries of science and technology, join PSB Academy’s SET and be a part of our network of engineers and technologists in Singapore!

School of Postgraduate Studies

Located right in the heart of Singapore, PSB Academy’s City Campus provides working professionals with a focal point on postgraduate and executive education. Together with globally leading universities and business schools – some of which are accredited by AACSB, AMBA and EQUIS – the School of Postgraduate Studies offers a comprehensive selection of MBAs, specialised Master’s Degrees, Doctoral Degrees, and more across 6 disciplines.

Dedicated to pushing boundaries in quality education, lessons are delivered by academics with curated learning materials. The 100,000 square feet City Campus @ Marina Square also incorporates specialised MBA seminar rooms with acoustically-treated walls and video conferencing capabilities to help forge a conducive learning environment where learners can advance their knowledge, communicate and build meaningful connections.

Regardless of your career aspirations, business growth or learning needs, you can browse and find a suitable full-time or part-time course that meets your requirements in the discipline of accounting & finance, analytics, business management, cyber security, engineering business management, logistics & supply chain management, and life sciences.

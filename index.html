WFI Education - A Leading Private Education Institution In Sri Lanka

Advance in your professional development journey with PSB Academy and be industry-ready at the end of your programme. Our six schools – which include School of Foundation Studies, School of Business and Management, School of Engineering and Technology, School of Health and Life Sciences, School of Postgraduate Studies and Digital Academy – will equip you with the necessary skills to take you places.

Serving students from over 50 nationalities, PSB Academy provides part-time and full-time degree and diploma courses, focusing on what really matters in our approach to education. Covering a comprehensive range of disciplines across different academic levels, explore how our qualifications may help you get where you want to be in your career.

Why Wfi Education?

1. Enriching Campus Life

To complement your education in Singapore, you can join student clubs for a holistic experience at PSB Academy. To learn more, get started on our virtual campus tour or feel free to chat with our students about the campus life here.

1. Focus on Employability

Upon graduation, one important aspect of a job application is your soft skills. These include your leadership and management abilities, problem-solving skills etc, which can be a major deciding factor. Our leadership development and mentoring programmes focus on developing your character and interpersonal skills to stand out from the rest.

Additionally, we will guide you through career preparation with our career services.

Enroll in WFI Education Today

At PSB Academy, we have diploma, degree and postgraduate courses for prospective students, available in both part-time and full-time study options. For more information, feel free to get in touch with us – we will respond to you shortly!
